**Talk**: Speak to someone or respond to conversation
- Use when: Customer speaks to you, greeting customers, answering questions, general conversation
- Parameters: [target_name, message_to_say] (string, string) - Who to talk to and what to say
- Example: ["Customer", "안녕하세요! 어서오세요!"], ["고객님", "네, 무엇을 도와드릴까요?"], ["손님", "감사합니다!"]
