**NotifyReceptionist**: Send a directed message to the receptionist
- **Use when**: You need the receptionist to relay information or prepare a patient
- **Effect**: Performs a remote, targeted Talk to the linked HospitalReceptionist (inspector reference)
- **Parameters**: [message] (string)
- **Examples**:
  - ["다음 환자분 안내 부탁드립니다"]
  - ["진료 준비 완료, 환자 입실 안내 바랍니다"]
