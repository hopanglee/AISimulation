- parameters: ["dish_key"]
- The dish_key must match a key registered in the inspector cookablePrefabs
- If hands are full, store in inventory; if everything is full, place it at the current location
- examples: ["Yakitori"], ["Oden"], ["Karaage"], ["Edamame"]
