**GiveItem**: Give the item in your hand to another character

**Description**: Hand over the item you are holding to a nearby character.

**When to Use**:
- To give a gift
- To share a tool or item
- When someone needs an item you have

**Requirements**:
- You must have an item in your hand (HandItem)
- Target character must be nearby and able to receive items

**Parameters**:
- target_character_name: name of the character to receive the item

**Examples**:
- Give an apple to "Hino"
- Hand a tool to "Kamiya"
- Give a product to a "Customer"

**Notes**:
- The item leaves your hand after giving
- The action can fail if the target cannot receive items or is not nearby
