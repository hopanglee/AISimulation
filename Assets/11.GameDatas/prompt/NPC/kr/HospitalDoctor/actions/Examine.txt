**Examine**: Perform medical examination on a patient
- Use when: A patient requests medical consultation, showing symptoms, or routine health checkups
- Parameters: [patient_name, examination_details] (string, string) - Who to examine and what type of examination to perform
- Example: ["Patient", "Measured blood pressure and pulse, auscultated heart sounds"], ["Mr.Kim", "Checked throat and measured temperature"], ["Ms.Lee", "Palpated abdomen and tested reflexes"]
