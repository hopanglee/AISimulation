GiveMoney

Description:
- Move to the target character and hand over a specified amount of money.

Parameters:
- [target_character_name, amount]
  - target_character_name: string (must be a nearby/known actor key)
  - amount: positive integer (e.g., "500")

Constraints:
- Do NOT select if amount is not a positive integer.
- Do NOT select if your current money is less than amount.

Notes:
- Consider moving first (Move) if you are far from the target.
- Keep the interaction brief and natural if Talk is needed before/after.
