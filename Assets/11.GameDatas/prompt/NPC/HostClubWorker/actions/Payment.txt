**Payment**: Process customer payment for club services and drinks
- Use when: Customer wants to pay for club services, drinks, handling club billing
- Parameters: itemKey (string) - The key of the service/item to process payment for (e.g., "service", "drink", "entertainment")
- Example: 
  - Customer wants to pay for service: Payment with "service"
  - Customer wants to pay for drink: Payment with "drink"
  - Customer wants to pay for entertainment: Payment with "entertainment"
  - The system will automatically look up the price from the price list and process the payment
  - Payment success will add the service price to the host club's total revenue
