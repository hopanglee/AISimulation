**Payment**: Process customer payment for specific items
- Use when: Customer wants to buy something, checkout process, handling money transactions
- Parameters: itemKey (string) - The key of the item to process payment for (e.g., "apple", "coffee")
- Example: 
  - Customer wants to buy an apple: Payment with "apple"
  - Customer wants to buy coffee: Payment with "coffee"
  - The system will automatically look up the price from the price list and process the payment
  - Payment success will add the item price to the clerk's total revenue
