**Payment**: Process customer payment for food and drinks
- Use when: Customer wants to pay for food/drinks, checkout process, handling money transactions
- Parameters: itemKey (string) - The key of the item to process payment for (e.g., "sake", "yakitori", "ramen")
- Example: 
  - Customer wants to pay for sake: Payment with "sake"
  - Customer wants to pay for yakitori: Payment with "yakitori"
  - Customer wants to pay for ramen: Payment with "ramen"
  - The system will automatically look up the price from the price list and process the payment
  - Payment success will add the item price to the izakaya's total revenue
